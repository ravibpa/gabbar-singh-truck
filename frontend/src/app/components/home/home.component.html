Our Story

From Punjab's
Streets to Yours

🌾

Gabbar Singh Truck was born from a simple idea: what if you could taste the real India — not the watered-down version, but the actual street food that makes your eyes water and your soul sing?

Our founder, Chef Gurpreet Singh, grew up eating his grandmother's Punjabi cooking and learning South Indian recipes from his college days in Chennai. He combined these two great traditions into one legendary food truck.

The name "Gabbar" isn't just a Bollywood reference — it's a promise. Gabbar doesn't apologize. Neither do our flavors.
